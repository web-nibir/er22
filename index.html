FAQ

Common Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our services.

We treat a wide range of conditions including back pain, neck pain, stroke rehabilitation, sports injuries, arthritis, post-surgical recovery, paralysis, sciatica, frozen shoulder, and pediatric developmental conditions. We also provide specialized rehabilitation for surgical patients including orthopedic, colorectal, and burn trauma cases.

No, you don't need a referral to visit our clinic. You can directly book an appointment. However, if you have medical reports or prescriptions from your doctor, please bring them as they help us understand your condition better.

Initial consultations typically take 45-60 minutes including assessment and treatment planning. Follow-up therapy sessions usually last 30-45 minutes depending on your treatment protocol. We ensure adequate time for quality care without rushing.

Physiotherapy should not be painful. You may experience mild discomfort during certain exercises or manual therapy techniques, but our therapists always work within your comfort level. We believe in gentle, progressive treatment that respects your body's limits.

Please wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy movement. For lower body conditions, bring shorts. For back or shoulder issues, wear a top that allows access to the treatment area. We also provide gown options if needed.

We work with several major health insurance providers in Bangladesh. Please contact us with your insurance details before your appointment so we can verify coverage. We also provide detailed invoices for reimbursement claims.
